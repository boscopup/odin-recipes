Mead of Poetry

When the Ã†sir-Vanir War ended, the gods of each group chewed strawberries and spat out their saliva to form Kvasir. Kvasir was extremely wise. Two dwarfs, Fjalar and Galar killed Kvasir and used his blood to create the Mead of Poetry, which gave any drinker wisdom.

Ingredients

  • Kvasir's blood
  • Honey
  • Water

Directions

  1. Kill Kvasir
  2. Drain Kvasir's blood into a vat of honey
  3. Pour mixture into water
  4. Expose to the sun for 40 days
  5. Place near a fire for at least 6 months
